What we're building

The SODO Track is transforming 2-miles of building backs into an epic street art corridor for 50,000 bus and light rail riders to experience as they enter Downtown Seattle each day.

Who is painting the walls?

Local, national and international artists are coming together to create an imaginative raceway of art in motion.
